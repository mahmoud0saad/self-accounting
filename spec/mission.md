# Mission — Daily Self-Accountability App (Muhasabah)

## Purpose
Help individual Muslim adults perform a consistent, lightweight **daily Muhasabah** (self-accountability) by turning their acts of worship and remembrance (Adhkar) into a trackable, motivating, gamified checklist.

## Vision
A calm, eye-friendly companion that turns intention into habit — where every day is measurable, every streak is meaningful, and self-improvement is visible.

## Target User
- **Primary persona:** An individual Muslim adult who already practices the daily prayers and Adhkar and wants a simple way to *measure* and *improve* consistency.
- **Single-user, personal device.** No social, community, or family-sharing features in scope for v1.
- Users may be online or offline; the app must remain useful in both states.

## Core Differentiator
**Gamification & streaks.** The app's identity centers on:
- A clear daily progress percentage (% of points completed).
- Streaks for consecutive days of high completion.
- Weekly/monthly visual progress to reinforce momentum.
- Points-weighted tasks so users instinctively learn which acts carry more spiritual weight (e.g., congregational prayer, Qiyam, Quran).

We are **not** competing on:
- Prayer-time accuracy (we integrate, we don't replace dedicated prayer apps).
- Quran reading (we track, we don't host the Mushaf).
- Social/community competition.

## Guiding Principles
1. **Niyyah-first, not guilt-first.** The tone of the UI, copy, and notifications is encouraging — never shaming.
2. **Calm by design.** Soft greens, generous spacing, no aggressive red error states. The app should feel like a prayer rug, not a productivity dashboard.
3. **Frictionless logging.** Marking a task complete must take ≤ 1 tap. No modals, no confirmations.
4. **The user owns their data.** Local-first behavior; cloud sync is optional and explicit.
5. **Honest measurement.** Points reflect Islamic priority (fard > sunnah > nafl), not arbitrary gamification.
6. **Privacy.** No tracking, no ads, no third-party analytics on worship data.

## Success Signals (v1)
- A user can complete their full daily checklist in under 60 seconds of cumulative interaction.
- A returning user maintains a 7-day streak within their first two weeks.
- Daily active retention ≥ 40% at week 4.

## Out of Scope (v1)
- Multi-user / family / community accounts.
- Leaderboards or social comparison.
- In-app Quran reader or Adhan player.
- Monetization (ads, subscriptions, in-app purchases).
